Choosing a used book

You receive a bonus after this survey depending on the ratio of the book's TRUE quality and price that you purchase. For example, a book that has a true quality score of 50 points and costs 10 dollars (ratio = 50:10 = 5) leads to a higher extra payment than a book that has a true quality score of 40 points and costs 10 dollars (ratio = 40:10 = 4). However, remember that the displayed quality score may differ from the true quality score.

Your task is to select the book that provides the highest true quality per dollar.

The extra payment for selecting the book with the highest true quality/dollar ratio is $ 0.40, for selecting the book with the second highest ratio it is $ 0.38, for selecting the book with the third highest score it is $ 0.36, etc.. If your purchase turns out to be the one with the lowest true quality/dollar ratio, $ 0.02 will be awarded.
